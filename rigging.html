Current Rigging

All standing rigging runs to turnbuckles

Standing Rigging

Shrouds and stays should be tensioned 5% to 20% of their rated breaking strength

Bullseye rigging 1/8" 1x19 SS wire breaking strength is 1760 lbs. (type 302 1/8 1x19 breaking strength 2100 lbs.)

tension procedure start all shrouds and stays at half design tension, then 3/4, gradually moving towards full tension.
