Folkboat Shopping List

Electrical

  1. multimeter
  2. battery monitor (Victron 712 $200)
  3. ring terminals heat shrink (Ancor from Hamilton)
  4. crimper, ring terminals (Ancorv 703010 $60)
  5. label maker (Dymo Rhino 4200 $80)
  6. heat shring tubing for labels (Dymo $30)
  7. heat gun (Wagner from Lowes $50)
  8. red/yellow wire ($2/foot at Hamilton)
  9. battery cables (Pacer)
  10. fuses
  11. fuse block
  12. bus bars (one positive, one negative)
  13. terminal strips
  14. cable tie mounts
  15. cable ties (every 18") or waxed linen for cable lacing
  16. battery box strap
  17. Marine GPS mount for GPSMAP 78-series ($25)

Safety

  1. Danforth 22S anchor 25# with 72' grade 30 chain 5/16" 6#
    • (Lorraine has) Danforth 13# and Danforth 8# and CQR 25#
    • (Folkboat International allows) 4.5kg aluminum anchor + 7.5kg chain
  2. Anchor rode (>25m/82ft at least 12mm)
  3. Anchor spurling pipe cover/plug
  4. Mooring lines @2 (20m total)
  5. Inflatable dinghy (Avon Redcrest? $400)
  6. Manual inflation pump
  7. Hand lead line - backup depth sounder
  8. Harness, tether, and jacklines ($300)
  9. Lofrans Royal Horizontal Manual Windlass ($1200)

Tools and spares (outboard)

  • pliers
  • socket wrench 10x13mm
  • spark plugsocket wrench 5/8" or 16mm
  • socket wrench handle
  • screwdrivers (philips and straight)
  • emergency outboard starter rope 36" or 100 mm
  • spare spark plug NGK: DCPR6E
  • spare split pin (propeller nut)
  • spare outboard stop switch lock/lanyard
